Problem Statement

Access Management

  • Inefficient entry/exit tracking for residents.
  • Manual and slow visitor registration process.

Service & Maintenance

  • Complex fee payment collection.
  • Unorganized terrace reservations for events.
  • Slow incident reporting and tracking.

Financial Management

  • Lack of transparency in fee usage.
  • Need for detailed reports on expenditures & invoices.

Our Solution

IoT Module

Automated access authentication using secure codes, interfacing directly with the digital access log.

Web Ecosystem

  • Admin Panel: Full control over condo management.
  • Security Portal: Guard interface for entries and detail logging.

Mobile App

Resident app to view activity, pay fees, reserve spaces, and generate access codes.

The Challenge

Fleet Visibility

  • Lack of real-time GPS tracking for active units.
  • Difficulty predicting estimated arrival times (ETA).

Driver Communication

  • Reliance on phone calls/WhatsApp for status updates.
  • Lost paperwork and delivery confirmations (PODs).

Data Silos

  • Maintenance logs separated from dispatch scheduling.
  • Inefficient reporting for client billing.

Our Solution

Central Dashboard

Real-time map view of all units with status, speed, and geofencing alerts.

Driver App

Mobile interface for route acceptance, digital POD scanning, and instant messaging.

Integrated CMS

Unified database connecting vehicle maintenance schedules with dispatch availability.
